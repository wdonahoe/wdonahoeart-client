Artist Statement

As a Realist Artist my intention is not to create art that merely records an image that will only elicit a singular response. The phrase, “a picture tells a thousand stories”, is how I approach art and create art, and that the conceptual elements will engage the viewer to lead them to their own personal response. What a tragedy that the artist would disclose their intent and in doing so erases the mystery. Allow the viewer to have their moment alone... find their own words.

Art is wordless but to ourselves.
